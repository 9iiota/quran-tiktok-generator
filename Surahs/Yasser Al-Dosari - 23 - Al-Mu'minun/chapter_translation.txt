And if you should obey a man like yourselves,
indeed, you would then be losers.
Does he promise you that when you have died
and become dust and bones
that you will be brought forth [once more]?
How far, how far,
How far, how far, is that which you are promised.
It [i.e., life] is not but our worldly life -
we die and live, but we will not be resurrected.
He is not but a man who has invented a lie about Allah,
and we will not believe him."
He said, "My Lord, support me because they have denied me."
